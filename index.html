Research

Check out my research page via web.illinois.edu →
Independent Study under Prof Andres Goza

In the Numerics and Unsteady Flows Group (NUF), I'm currently working towards developing an existing numerical method into a higher order method of the same algorithm.

Independent Study (AE 497) under Prof Marco Panesi

At the Center for Hypersonics & Entry Systems Studies (CHESS), I'm currently working towards fitting the kinetics data of hypersonic reentry flight using sparse regression techniques to obtain an expression for the state-to-state dissociation rates in terms of the position of the centrifugal barrier. By selecting a large basis set of functions that include exponentials and polynomials motivated by derivations from transition state theory, we can model the chemical reactions that take place in the flow.

Cubesat I (ENG 491) under Prof Michael Lembeck

At the Laboratory for Advanced Space Systems at Illinois (LASSI), I've managed various teams from mission operations to the entire satellite program after initially assisting with structural CAD tasks and systems engineering with proposals and requirements documents.
