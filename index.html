Laboratory for Advanced Space Systems at Illinois

At LASSI, I'm currently working with attitude determination and control systems of cubesats. In the past, I've managed various teams from mission operations to the entire satellite program after initially assisting with structural CAD tasks and systems engineering with proposals and requirements documents.

Learn more about LASSI →
Non-Equilibrium Kinetics and Radiative Gas Dynamics Laboratory

At NEQRAD, I'm currently working towards fitting the kinetics data of hypersonic reentry flight using sparse regression techniques to obtain an expression for the state-to-state dissociation rates in terms of the position of the centrifugal barrier. By selecting a large basis set of functions that include exponentials and polynomials motivated by derivations from transition state theory, we can model the chemical reactions that take place in the flow.

Learn more about NEQRAD →
