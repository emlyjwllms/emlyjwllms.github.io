Emily Williams
I am a graduate student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Aeronautics and Astronautics with a specialization in Computational Science and Engineering. I am working with Dr. Adrián Lozano-Durán on physics-based and data-driven modeling for computational fluid dynamics of turbulence. I completed my undergraduate degree in Aerospace Engineering with a minor in Atmospheric Sciences and a concentration in Computational Science and Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in May 2021. My goal is to employ machine learning to model hypersonic flows, which involves complex aerothermodynamics and numerical methods.
